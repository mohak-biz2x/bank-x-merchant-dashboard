Security Cheque Submission

To activate your drawdown facility, you are required to physically send a security cheque of AED 5,500,000 (approved limit + 10%) to Mal Bank. Your financing facility will remain on hold until the cheque is received and verified by our operations team.

Once Mal Bank confirms receipt of your security cheque, your drawdown facility will be enabled and you will be notified via email and within the portal.

Processing may take 2–3 business days after receipt.
